# Ultra-Deep Archive of "Received" Languages
### Extended Catalogue of Spirit-Attributed, Visionary, Alien, Dream, and Trance Languages

This archive expands the earlier lists to include **obscure historical, occult, mediumistic, shamanic, and contactee languages**.

A “received language” here means the originator claimed the language was:

- revealed
- transmitted
- channeled
- remembered from a previous existence
- taught by spirits or non-human beings
- encountered in dreams or altered states

Many examples are **fragmentary**, consisting only of vocabulary lists, ritual phrases, or invented alphabets.
